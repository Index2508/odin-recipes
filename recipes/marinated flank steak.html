Marinated flank steak

odin recipes

Description

Marinated flank steak is a flavorful and tender cut of beef that has been soaked in a mixture of various ingredients to enhance its taste and texture. Flank steak comes from the abdominal region of the cow and is known for its long, flat shape and pronounced grain.

marinating process involves immersing the flank steak in a blend of liquids and seasonings, allowing the flavors to penetrate and infuse the meat. The marinade typically consists of acidic ingredients like vinegar or citrus juice, which help to tenderize the meat, along with oil, herbs, spices, and sometimes soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce for added depth of flavor.

The marinating time can vary depending on the recipe, but it often ranges from 30 minutes to several hours. During this time, the flavors from the marinade work their way into the meat, making it more flavorful and succulent.

Once the marinating is complete, the flank steak can be cooked using various methods, such as grilling, broiling, or pan-searing. It is essential to cook it quickly over high heat to retain its tenderness and juiciness. After cooking, the meat should be sliced against the grain, which helps to break up the muscle fibers and further enhances its tenderness.

Marinated flank steak is a popular choice for barbecues, cookouts, and gatherings due to its delicious taste and versatility. It can be served as a main dish alongside various sides, sliced for sandwiches or tacos, or added to salads for a satisfying and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

    Marinade:

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

    • Steak:

  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak

Directions

    Step 1

ather all ingredients.

    Step 2

Whisk together oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper for marinade until thoroughly combined. Place steak in a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.

    Step 3

Pour marinade over flank steak in the baking dish; turn several times to coat thoroughly with marinade. Cover, and refrigerate for 2 to 6 hours, or up to 12 hours if you have time.

    Step 4

When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.

    Step 5

Remove steak from the marinade and shake off excess. Discard the remaining marinade.

    Step 6

Cook steak on the preheated grill for about 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

    Step 7

Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

    Step 8

Serve hot and enjoy!
